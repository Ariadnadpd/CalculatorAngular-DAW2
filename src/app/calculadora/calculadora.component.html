The calculator is developed with version 13.2.6 of the angular framework. Its design is based on the calculator provided in the macOS operating system.


The calculator shows at the top the operation performed and at the bottom the indicated operation and then its result.


IMPORTANT: To make use of the calculator correctly you have to click right in the center of the number or symbol.
